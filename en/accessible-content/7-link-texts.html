How to write descriptive link texts

Don't use "Click here"

Looking for a nice holiday? Click here for holiday homes by the Wadden Sea.

  • For information about seals, click here
  • For information about seagulls, click here
  • For information about mudflat walking, click here

Use descriptive text instead

Looking for a nice holiday? View our selection of Wadden Sea holiday homes.

  • Seals
  • Seagulls
  • Mudflat walking
