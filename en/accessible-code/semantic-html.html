  1. Heat a knob of butter in a 20 cm diameter frying pan and add a tablespoon of batter.
  2. Turn the pan around so that the entire bottom is covered.
  3. Let the pancake bake for 2 minutes over medium heat until the top is dry and the bottom is golden brown.
  4. Turn the pancake over and bake for 1 more minute.
