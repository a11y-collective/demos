Youtube embeds

Embed code out of the YouTube box

<iframe
	width="560"
	height="315"
	src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/_Z5vPFc3PJ8"
	frameborder="0"
	allow="accelerometer;
	autoplay; clipboard-write;
	encrypted-media;
	gyroscope;
	picture-in-picture"
	allowfullscreen
>
</iframe>

With a title and with shortcuts disabled

<iframe
	width="560"
	height="315"
	src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/_Z5vPFc3PJ8?disablekb=1"
	title="Rian Rietveld, Who's afraid of ARIA"
	frameborder="0"
	allow="accelerometer;
	clipboard-write;
	encrypted-media;
	gyroscope;
	picture-in-picture"
	allowfullscreen
	>
</iframe>
